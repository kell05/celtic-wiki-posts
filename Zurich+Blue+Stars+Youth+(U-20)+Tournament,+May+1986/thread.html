Just seen this. I've had a scan through John Litster's A RECORD OF POST-WAR SCOTTISH LEAGUE PLAYERS CD and come up with some ideas:

Connelly - A goalie (Steve) Connelly signed for St. Mirren in 1987 and went on to get games for Albion Rovers in 91/92. He came from Thorniewood Jnrs but for all I know might have been a CBCer before that. No d.o.b. given.

Dean (or "Dino", if its the same guy) Connelly was definately a defender who went off to Arsenal then Barnsley, Wigan etc.

McKeevor - A Russell McKeever is quoted at Queens Park from 20.2.91 after signing from Hamilton Thistle. But his d.o.b. is given as 17.1.73 making him 13 in '86. ??

Walsh - played twice for Ayr then untraced. Poss. Ayrshire Juniors?

Robertson - Not convinced it was the soon-to-be-hun Robertson. Sralex had him on an S-form from 13 or so I read and he debuted for Aberdeen a few weeks later in August 86, having been with Deeside BC prior to that.

Conlan - the only Conlan the right sort of age was Peter (b. 21.6.70) who played for Ayr, Glenafton, Beith etc. He'd be very young then though - not even 16.
