Trivia

  • With no injuries reported Jock Stein fielded the same side and set up as used against Ayr Utd in the league the previous week.
  • Hibs anger Celtic fans by putting the admission prices up by 10 pence.pence in the Ground and nearly double the price in the Stand.
  • Celtic draw Clydebank in the next round.
  • Highlights shown next day on Scotsport.
  • McGrain, Dalglish and Wilson named in the Scotland squad for the game against Spain on February 5th.
