Tom White

Celtic Board - Past | Celtic Board | Celtic's Foundation | About Celtic

Director Tom White

A lawyer and newspaper director Tom White replaced John Glass on the Celtic board in 1906. He would serve on the board until 1947.

His son Desmond White would also go on to be a director and chairman of the club, whilst his grandson Chris White would also be a director of the club (albeit a member of the infamous "Biscuit Tin" board of the 1990s).
