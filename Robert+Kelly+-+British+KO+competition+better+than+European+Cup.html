Robert Kelly - British KO competition better than European Cup

Sir Robert Kelly Biog | Celtic Board - Past | About Celtic

Interview with Celtic Chairman Robert Kelly, July 1968.
Published in the Irish News, the interview was facilitated by Charlie Tully (pictured with Kelly) and took place at the O'Reilly home in Cushendall, Co. Antrim, where Kelly was holidaying
