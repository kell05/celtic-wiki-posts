Player of the Month 2007/08
August 2007

With 2 MOTM performances and 1 Second place, Massimo Donati topped the points table and is KDS Player of the Month for August.

September 2007
 2 MOTM performances and 1 second place, just like Donati last month, meant Scott McDonald topped the table this time and is KDS Player of the Month for September.
October 2007
2 MOTM displays against Gretna and Motherwell and 2 second places against AC Milan and Rangers this month makes sure that Scott McDonald, for the 2nd month in a row, gets the Player of the Month for October.
November 2007
 Consistency, including a MOTM performance against Benfica was enough for young Aiden McGeady to win the KDS Player of the Month for November.
December 2007
3 MOTM displays, 2 second places and a third place, in the 8 games played last month, and Aiden McGeady easily wins the KDS Player of the Month for December.
January 2008

 Only 3 games were played in January, but Massimo Donati topped the points table to become the KDS player of the Month for January.
February 2008
For the 3rd time in 4 months, Aiden McGeady is the KDS Player of the Month for February.
March 2008
 3 MOTM performances this month makes Andreas Hinkel the KDS Player of the Month for March.
April 2008
4 top 3 finishes in the 5 games played in sees Barry Robson named as the KDS Player of the Month for April
May 2008
 Only 3 games played this month, Barry Robson has now finished in the top 3 in each of our last 7 games, and is the KDS Player of the Month for May
