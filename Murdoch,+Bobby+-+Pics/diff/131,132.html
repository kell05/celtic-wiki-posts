This photo was taken during the second half (old South Stand visible in background) with Celtic attacking the Hun end at Hampden. Big Billy scored in the first minute shooting into the Celtic end. the head butt occurred mid way - certainly not in the early stages - during the first half while Rangers were attacking the Celtic goal - the stand in the background is the old South Stand and Celtic were defending the goal at the Rangers end. The score at the time was still 1-0 - but not for long - as the song goes. Alex Ferguson's Last game for Rangers.
