Rodgers, Brendan - Quotes

Homepage

Quotes

"I will give everything I have and do all I can to bring our supporters exciting, entertaining and winning football. The club has been in magnificent shape in recent years and has collected silverware regularly during this time. My objective now, of course, is to continue this work, to keep us at the top and again make our mark in Europe."
Brendan Rodgers (on appointment, May 2016)

“I have followed Celtic all my life and to be given this fantastic opportunity and to be part of such a truly great football club is a dream come true."
Brendan Rodgers (on appointment, May 2016)

“Most of us grew up with Brendan Rodgers. He is just known around here as Fudgie. We used to play football with him in the car park or behind St John’s Primary school. I was in his class at primary school and when the teacher asked what he wanted to be when he grew up, Fudgie would say ‘a rally driver or to play for Celtic'. We were very proud when he managed Liverpool but there are even more Celtic fans in Carnlough and the place is just buzzing.”
Gary Morgan, of Carnlough Celtic Travel Club (May 2016)

“It shows you what the world of football is now, the gossip and speculation that goes on. I’ve just landed my dream job. The team I’ve supported all my life. I haven’t even walked out for my first game."
Brendan Rodgers (June 2016)

"Celtic is always the big attraction, the big draw, the big club, we are very proud to take Celtic around the world for our supporters and players. Every game is a big game. There is no game that is any bigger or smaller.Of course, you have different level of opponent, but, for myself and the staff, that doesn’t change in terms of our preparation and at Celtic there is a huge expectation on us to do well. So, for us, it doesn’t change."
Brendan Rodgers (July 2016)

“A lot has been said about what has happened in the past but you have to embrace failure,” he said. “That’s how you succeed. If you run away from it and be frightened of it then you’re never going to succeed.“If you trace or track any teams, coaches or players and look at the things they have won or the success they’ve had, if you look below that you’ll see failures as well so you have to embrace it and work even harder to achieve what it is you want to achieve."
Brendan Rodgers (Aug 2016)

“You are either a coach that waits, or creates,” Rodgers said. “And I come from the creative side. So I’ll never die wondering, or waiting. I always think, whether it is in life or whether it is in football, if you rely on yourself, then you can be happy. Because at least if you fail, you fail on your terms. So that’s by creating, right. If I wait, I’m reliant on someone else. In life, you only rely on yourself, and the team and the people around you."
Brendan Rodgers (Aug 2016)

"I know Celtic is not just a football club it's a way of life. You must watch this. You'll feel inspired."
Brendan Rodgers (Aug 2016)

"For me, whatever I do in the rest of my life and my career, to have managed Celtic is the ultimate, for me. I'm hopefully going to be working in football for the next 15 to 20 years but to have managed Glasgow Celtic and been here is an incredible honour.
"I can only do my best, the club is based around winning trophies. Playing football like the way Celtic supporters enjoy which is offensive, attacking football but that always has to be with discipline and order. Win as many trophies as we possibly can and hopefully whenever I leave here, whenever that is and hopefully it's not going to be for a long time the legacy left is the football and the feeling that people got from the inside of what football gave them, that when they went home of an evening or after games it was something that made them happy."
Brendan Rodgers in interview with Celtic Underground (Sep 2016)

"The best way of keeping your critics quiet is to win games of football"
Brendan Rodgers (Oct 2016)

"Celtic are lucky to have this guy as manager. Gives his team belief, purpose, conviction. More good signs tonight."
Graham Speirs (Journalist, Oct 2016)

“I don’t think there is any arrogance in what’s been said. I want them to have a football arrogance.”
Brendan Rodgers (Nov 2016)

"For the people I love and the Club I support"
Brendan Rodgers (Nov 2016)

"I don't see Celtic as a stepping stone, I want to be here as long as I can."
Brendan Rodgers (Nov 2016)

"The competition this year was about improvement and I think we can see that."
Brendan Rodgers

"Coming to Ibrox isn't just about the points, it's about defending the culture of our club."
Brendan Rodgers (Jan 2017)

“The best time to improve is when you are successful. As a football club you can’t sit and glorify."
Brendan Rodgers (Feb 2017)

"There is a whole load for us still to do. That’s the exciting part - because we have only just started.”
Brendan Rodgers (Feb 2017)

"The team are starting to have this priceless quality that you need in football which is to stay calm & find a way to win."
Brendan Rodgers (Feb 2017)

“There is not a place I could be in this world right now where I’d be happier in my football life and personal life."
Brendan Rodgers (Apr 2017)

"This is Celtic - this is one of the great, iconic clubs of the world."
Brendan Rodgers (Apr 2017)

"I'm a Celtic supporter & it's a wee bit surreal winning the league as coach after watching for yrs. I'll have a cup of tea tonight"
Brendan Rodgers (Apr 2017)

"For me, Celtic is the greatest club in the world"
Brendan Rodgers (Apr 2017)

"I've come to Celtic to build something special here. I'm really happy in my life and want to keep developing the club"
Brendan Rodgers (Apr 2017)

“I’m proud that my philosophy of attacking with aggression and entertaining supporters is followed through."
Brendan Rodgers (May 2017)

"I was born into Celtic. It’s a huge privilege to manage this club. I was born into it and we have created something which hopefully allows us to build on this. To do what we did this season is spectacular."
Brendan Rodgers answers questions at end of Scottish Cup final, having just completed the treble (May 2017)

"'Football people will wonder what Dave King is talking about. I don’t need Dave King to motivate me or the players. Our motivation is for Celtic."
Brendan Rodgers (2017)

"I have a team that knows how to play football now."
Brendan Rodgers (Aug 2017)

Stevie Gerrard(ex-Liverpool player under Rodgers): "Would you relish Anfield return?"
Brendan Rodgers: "They must be paying you to say that!"
Brendan Rodger interviewed by his ex-player on BT Sport after 5-0 victory over Astana in European Cup, on Celtic possibility of Celtic drawing Liverpool in Group Stages(Aug 2017)

“He’s top drawer. He’s very slick on the training ground, he’s a fantastic man manager, he makes you feel good and confident all the time. He helped me an awful lot. People might not think that but he helped break my game down and change my game so you’ve got to give him a lot of credit.”
Stevie Gerrad (ex-Liverpool player) (2017)

"If you're in a race, you never look back, you always look forward. The only club I ever worry about is Celtic."
Brendan Rodgers (2017)

"[Brendan Rodgers'] players get to wear a jersey with a European star, PSG’s do not."
Line from article in The Times Sep 2017 on Brendan Rodgers' Celtic side to face PSG in the European Cup

“The potential here is huge. But it’s about steps. Listen, there is a star outside the ground because we have won it."
Brendan Rodgers (Sep 2017)

“We play teams four to six times a year and Celtic always have to be dominant. But it can never be stale. You can’t be predictable. And having the flexibility to play in the different systems that we do will hopefully make it challenging for our opponents each time we play. So today playing with the two strikers, with the width and the numbers inside – it worked well."
Brendan Rodgers (Sep 2017)

“You have to be careful with young players. I say to the young players here, it’s very easy to walk around with the Louis Vuitton soap bag. But when you walk out of here, don’t tell people you play for Celtic. No, you are only training with Celtic. You are playing for Celtic when you are in the first team. Lots of young players these days look good, they smell nice, but they don’t put the work in. They don’t play. It’s about working to play for the first team."
Brendan Rodgers (Sep 2017)

"In the last year we've written history at this club in terms of a treble and going unbeaten. But let's keep rewriting history."
Brendan Rodgers (Oct 2017)

“You learn with experience you will have good days and bad days. The bad days happen in football, but it’s about how you react. It’s creating a culture. As long as you have honest people and honest players around you, then you can’t complain.The criticism usually comes from the people who do not have that commitment.”
Brendan Rodgers (Nov 2017)

"Nothing should be written against our supporters and players about honesty. These guys have this record because of their honesty, because of how hard they work, because they never have a lazy day."
Brendan Rodgers (Dec 2017)

“You saw the response of the team on Wednesday night. It is all part of the culture we have here. We are very lucky, we have got no d***heads."
Brendan on the side after winning two games following end of the 69 match defeated run (Dec 2017)

"When you're an Irishman and you get your first goal for Celtic, it runs deep. This is a special day for him and his family."
Brendan on Jonny Hayes (Dec 2017)

"If it wasn't for Brendan, then I know I would not be the same player that you see at Barcelona today. Such a big part of my education is down to him and his management."
Luis Suarez

Stoke fan to BT Sport: “Brendan Rodgers is my pick for Stoke Manager.”
Steve McManaman(ex-Liverpool, now BT Sports analyst analyst): “Brendan wouldn’t go anywhere near the Stoke job. Why would you leave Celtic when you’re flying, winning trophy after trophy, to go to Stoke?”
BT Sports (Jan 2018)

"This is the game they were cheering for. For us, we always respect every opponent we play and get ready for the game. One of the values of this club is about humility. We respect every opponent we play. We just prepare for the game."
Brendan Rodgers (Apr 2018)

"We are at a club here where the history is enormous and what we did last year was historic but we want to keep writing our own history and that means going into these games focused on the next game."
Brendan Rodgers (Apr 2018)

“There’s always speculation but my only focus is Celtic. [If an approach came] I would speak to the board. It will take something extraordinary to take me away from here.”
Brendan Rodgers (Apr 2018)

"I woke up here on a Monday morning as the manager of Celtic, believe it or not the sky is blue here in Glasgow and I’m in the best job in the world.
I absolutely love it here. I’m so happy here at Celtic. I’m in a job that I love."
Brendan Rodgers (Apr 2018)

"We wanted seven, but we'll take five"
Brendan Rodgers following the 5-0 victory over Sevco at Celtic Park in the League (May 2018)

"To be in with a chance and opportunity to add another chapter to the great story of Celtic. That’s always been my main aim, to make the supporters proud and inspire them."
Brendan Rodgers (May 2018)

"For teams like ourselves, it's not so much the punches to the head and body that stop you, it's the pats on the back. So we haven't given out too many of them and we have to continue to work."
Brendan Rodgers (2018)

'Brendan Rodgers? I trust him with my life,'
Scott Brown on Rodgers (Aug 2018)

"It’s no good if I just sit back and get comfortable. Being comfortable is the enemy of progress."
Brendan Rodgers (Aug 2018)

“In Glasgow, only one team can be doing well. You’ve got to make sure that’s you."
Brendan Rodgers

"It’s one of the most intimidating arenas in European football. Some of the best teams in the world have came to Celtic Park over the years and suffered. It was a really emotional game, our players fed off that and they helped us to the victory."
Brendan Rodgers (Nov 2018)

During Celtic AGM: Fan worried that we are away to Hibs, Aberdeen and Sevco in December, Brendan replies to him: (Nov 2018)
'Don't worry. Just relax.'

"If you're happy in your work and you have good energy, there's a good chance you will be successful, and for me that place is here at Celtic"
Brendan Rodgers (Dec 2018)

"I have compassion for players, especially those that have done amazing for me, but what I don’t have is sentimentality."
Brendan Rodgers (Dec 2018)

“There have been a couple of drones spotted and I’ve seen a couple of guys walking their dogs. Is there a vantage point from the castle? Not with our snipers positioned up there!”
Brendan Rodgers on 'Spygate' after Leeds Utd manager got caught sending staff to spy on opposition team training (Jan 2019)

"Well it was a very, very difficult decision. If I was making this decision with my heart, I’d be at Celtic all my life. It all happened very quickly. I was presented with an opportunity, and of course emotionally I have to take all of that out.
“After almost three years at Celtic, after all we had achieved on and off the pitch, that it was time to move on to my next challenge. I’ve had opportunities to move, but this was an opportunity I felt was too good to turn down.
“Celtic are a huge club. But, it’s not the case of whether it’s bigger, it’s about coming to the Premier League. The challenge of coming in and working against top players and top coaches. That was the real draw.”
Brendan Rodgers on leaving Celtic (Mar 2019)

"Brendan Rodgers has been phenomenal for Celtic but what a little rat he is..."
Ex-Celt John Hughes on the @Stephen4_2 & @celticrumours podcast

“I had family who were just distraught I left Celtic, just couldn’t believe it. I have relations that haven’t spoken to me since the day I left Celtic. Because it meant so much to them. And if you carry on like that too much, you’ll never do anything. But I had some unforgettable memories at Celtic that will live with me for a lifetime, that I still think about now and I then came into my next experience."
Brendan Rodgers (May 2020)
