Teams

Esson, McNaughton, Winter, Diamond, Smith, Nicholson, Clark, Byrne, Muirhead, Considine, Crawford.
Goal:- Winter 53.


Celtic team:-
Boruc, Telfer, Balde, McManus, Wallace, Nakamura, Petrov, Lennon, McGeady, Zurawski (Maloney), Hartson (Virgo).
