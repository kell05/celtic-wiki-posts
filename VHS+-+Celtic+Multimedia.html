1982


The Celtic Story, 1888 - 1967:
Take 2 Video, 1982; later re-issued by Celtic Video.

The Sunday Post from January 16th 1983 reported this about The Celtic Story at the time -





Celtic v Inter Milan, European Cup final 1967:

Vertex, 1982, in their ‘Soccer Classics’ series. This video features the BBC coverage of the match in black-and-white.


1984



Celtic v Rangers, League Match at Parkhead, 2nd April 1984:

Celtic Video, and filmed for the club by Classic Video. No commentary is
provided, but the Jungle resounds in full voice as background to Celtic’s 3—0 victory. In a unique piece of Old Firm history, the video captures the
two goals scored by brothers Paul and Willie McStay.

1985


Celtic — Behind Closed Doors

Celtic Video, and filmed for Celtic F.C. by Classic Video, 1985. “Behind the scenes at Celtic Park . . . from preseason training to Cup Final preparation”, the video is a forerunner of the 1990s The Celtic Collection.


1986
Kenny Dalglish, Portrait of a Natural Footballer:


Castle Vision, 1986. The outstanding career of the Celtic, Liverpool, and Scotland superstar is charted in detail.

1988


The Official History of Celtic Football Club,


1989


Celtic F.C. 1988—1989, the Season’s Greatest League Highlights


Video Collection International Ltd., in their ‘International Sport Showcase’ series, and produced for the Scottish Football League, 1989.

1990


Hail! Hail! Old Firm Classics - Celtic


Pickwick Video, 1990. “Over 25 years of triumph from the greatest club game in the world. . . A priceless collection of Celtic’s most satisfying victories over their old rivals.”

.Manchester United v Celtic, Bryan Robson Testimonial at Old Trafford, November 20th 1990:
BA’ Sportscast, with Ron Jones and Denis Law commentating on Celtic’s 3—1 win.





1991

The Packie Bonner Story:


Sports Business Scotland, 1991. The Celtic and Eire goalkeeper tells his own story.

The Celtic Collection, Volume I:



Classic Video Productions Ltd., 1991. The first in a planned series of official quarterly video ‘magazines’, designed “to try and keep the Celtic supporter better informed than any other supporter in football” through interviews with the manager, players
etc. and through general ‘behind-the-scenes’ information. Features an interview with Liam Brady, and the pre-season goals from Ireland, as well as action from Arsenal & Spurs.
55 mins

1992

The Celtic Collection, Volume 2:


The second in the series, and produced in 1992.


Scottish Cup Greatest Victories Volume I: Celtic


Watershed Pictures Ltd., 1992. The video — one of an official SFA series — comprises highlights from Celtic’s triumphs in the 1977, 1980, 1985, and 1988 finals
(and also the semi-final victory over Hearts in 1988).

Tennent’s Scottish Cup semi-final, Celtic v Rangers, March 31st 1992:



Another in the above SFA series, it shows Celtic’s 0-1 loss to their rivals.


Lionhearts


Pender Productions, for Celtic F.C., 1992. Footage from
every round of Celtic’s triumphant European campaign in 1966/67, and
with comments by Liam Brady about the Lisbon final. Extra material
includes footage of the pre-season friendly against Manchester United
(with Best, Law, and Chariton), and the di Stefano testimonial after
Lisbon — and the farewell of the Lisbon Lions against Clyde in 1971.



Top Action from the 1991/92 Scottish League Season, Featuring Celtic F.C.

Castle Vision, for the official Scottish Football League video series,
1992.

1993

Celtic - Classic Celtic



Classic Celtic action from four decades of glory. Includes a 7-1 win over Rangers in '57, many victories and cup successes including the Double in 1988, the Centenary Year. Players include McNeill, McGrain, Johnstone, Deans, McClair, McStay and many more.
63 mins

1994

If You Know The History



Take a journey through the back pages of Celtic Football Club and re-live special moments as well as looking forward to the completion of the new Celtic Park.

1995




We're Back

On this gala day, the new North Stand at Celtic Park was officially opened by Rod Stewart. To commemmorate the event Celtic played Kevin Keegan's Newcastle United. Five days later they faced Roy Evans' Liverpool. We see here highlights of both of these games..

1996


The Maestro


Paul McStay made his debut for Celtic at the age of 17 and, over fifteen years later, he remains a Parkhead favourite. This video pays tribute to one of the club's most faithful servants, from early beginnings in a schoolboy international to his instalment as captain of the team he had followed from childhood.


The Celtic Diary

A review of the first half of the 96/97 season at Celtic Park with over 60 minutes of action, gossip, and interviews with Alan Stubbs and Paulo Di Canio. Youth Team Coach Willie McStay talks about the Youth Development Policy and two youngsters discuss their experiences with the first team.

1997


The Bhoys are Back…



1999






Larsson: The Players's Player



Highlights from the 1998/99 season at Celtic Football Club and Henrik Larsson as he became the country's top goal scorer.




2000


Celtic FC - 2000 And Won! - Celtic 2 - Aberdeen 0


The story of Celtic's 2000 CIS League Cup victory.
