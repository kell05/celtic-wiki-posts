Trivia

  • Pat McCluskey was out having been subbed in the weekend game with a chest injury. MacDonald was still out with an ankle injury. In came George Connelly from the start with Roy Aitken and Hood on the bench.
  • In fall-out from Scotland's international visit to Denmark the SFA slapped life bans on several players after misdemeanoiurs in the Scotland camp in Copenhagen. One of those was U23 and Celtic's Pat McCluskey. The other players involved were Billy Bremner, Joe Harper of Hibernian and Willie Young and Arthur Graham of Aberdeen.
  • Celtic's first visit to Ochilview since 1937.
  • Roy Aitken makes his Celtic debut as a sub.sub at the age of 16 and 11 months.
  • Stenny open a cash turnstile on the night when ticket sales are lower than expected.

Review

Celtic make a very rare trip to Ochilview Park.

Scotland manager Willie Ormond must be impressed by Dalglish's goal when he dummies Stenny defenders to create space to fire in an unstoppable 18 yard shot. Edvaldsson is a stand out in the game but is subbed to give Roy Aitken his debut.
No scoring before half time after Celtic had started brightly but then lost their way.
Lennox got the first heading home a Tommy Callaghan corner. And Dalglish's magnificent goal finished it off.
