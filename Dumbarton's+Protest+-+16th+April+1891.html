Dumbarton's Protest

16th April 1891


From a newspaper report.


Dumbarton's Protest - The Scottish Football League Committee were called together on Wednesday night in Glasgow to consider a protest from Dumbarton, who claimed that the referee in their League match with Celtic last Saturday erred in disallowing a goal scored by Bell, whom he adfudged offside. After consideration the matter was unanimously dismissed.
