February 2009
There were 5 matches played this month...

01-Feb-09 ICT 0-0 Celtic SPL McGeady - Boruc - Crosas
07-Feb-09 Celtic 2-1 Queens Park SC 5th McGeady - Hartley - Caldwell
15-Feb-09 Celtic 0-0 Rangers SPL Boruc - Caldwell - Nakamura
22-Feb-09 Motherwell 1-1 Celtic SPL Nakamura - McDonald - Caldwell
28-Feb-09 Celtic 7-0 St Mirren SPL Nakamura - Crosas - Brown S


(Result on KDS)
Shunsuke Nakamura played in 333 of the 450 minutes of football this month, he scored 3 goals, a hat-trick in the 7-0 win over St Mirren on the last day of the month.
His 2 MOTM performances plus a 3rd place against the huns gave him 7pts, all of which earns him the KDS Player of the Month for February
