History
F.C. Copenhagen or F.C. København is a Danish football team, playing in Copenhagen, and is now a part of Parken Sport & Entertainment. They have won the Danish championship on five occasions (1993, 2001, 2003, 2004 and 2006) and the Danish Cup on three occasions (1995, 1997 and 2004).

F.C. Copenhagen won the inaugural Royal League tournament in 2004-05, after tying IFK Göteborg in the final 1-1 and beating them in a penalty shootout, 12-11.
They won also the second season of the tournament, after this time winning against Lillestrøm S.K. in the final 1-0.
