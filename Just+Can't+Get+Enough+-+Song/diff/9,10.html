Background

"Just Can't Get Enough" was the third UK single by the popular 1980's synth pop group Depeche Mode. It was originally released in the UK in September 1981, and was also the band's first single to be released in the United States, on February 18, 1982. It was recorded during the summer of 1981. Popular in retro discos and for old nostalgics.
