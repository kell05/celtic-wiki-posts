Campbell, Johnny - Grave

Celtic Graves | Player Biog

Location of grave: Buried in Dalbeth Cemetery
Note: His Resting Place is in the West Plot - Lair No.214 (the same area of the cemetery as Jimmy McMenemy).

CGS Rememberance Service August 2015





Pre-2015




Celtic Graves Society commemoration.

Saturday 15th August 2015.
Guests included Tomas Tadia of the Polish Consulate, members of the Polska #1 CSC, Tony Hamilton, representatives of Third Lanark, David Potter and Chris Curry who spoke on behalf of the family of Johnny Campbell.
Father White of St Mary of the Assumption, Calton, Glasgow conducted the blessing.
