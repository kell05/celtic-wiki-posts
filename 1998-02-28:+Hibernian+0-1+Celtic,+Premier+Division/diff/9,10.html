Trivia

  • McNamara and Stubbs passed late fitness tests to make the game. Johnson was out with what turned out to be a cruciate ligament problem, Annoni came into the squad and Hann ah and MacKay dropped out.
  • Jim Duffy had been sacked as Hibernian manager on 2/2/98 and Alex McLeish appointed. His idea was to attempt to salvage Hibs season and keep them up for the new Premier League the following season. To do this he appeared to be applying a ‘kick-anything-in-sight’ tactical game which he had refined at Motherwell.
  • Hibs captain and ex-Celt John Hughes ended up with a four game ban following comments made to the linesman at the end of the game. His complaint was about why Stubbs challenge on the goalkeeper was not called as a foul which would have negated the goal.


Review

A tight game where we started well but let Hibs back into it. This kept us at the top and ahead on points after Rangers and Hearts drew against each other.
