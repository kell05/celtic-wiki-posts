1978-10-14: Dundee United 1-0 Celtic, League

Match Pictures | Matches: 1978 - 1979 | 1978-79 Pictures

Trivia

  • Tommy Burns continued in the unusual position of left back with the absence through injury of Andy Lynch. Both Conn and Glavin had taken knocks in the midweek League Cup game against Motherwell and Glavin was out. Roy Aitken captained the side.
  • Highlights shown on Scotsport next day.
  • On the same day Celtic Reserves lost 3 - 0 to Rangers Reserves at Celtic Park in the Second Eleven Cup. The Celtic team was Baines, Sneddon, Russell, McGrain, Casey, Weir, Doyle, Lumsden, O'Donnell, McPhee, Godzik Subs Ward and Halpin.


Review

Celtic never recovered from Kopel's first minute call and the fine early season form is fast disappearing. Latchford was late getting down to a Kopel shot and the ball got through. That was the trigger for a fierce game. Despite pushing Celtic could not find a goal and also had two penalty appeals turned down. The Arabs also had their chances and Latchford made amends for the opener by making a string of good saves.


Teams

Dundee Utd:
McAlpine, Stewart, Kopel, Fleming, Hegarty, Narey, Smith, Sturrock, Payne, Kirkwood, Addison. Subs: Robinson, Dodds
Scorer: Kopel (2)

Celtic:
Latchford, Filippi, Burns, Aitken, MacDonald, Edvaldsson (Mackie), Provan, Conroy, McAdam, Conn (G McCluskey), Lennox.

Referee: W Anderson (East Kilbride)
Attendance: 17,700
