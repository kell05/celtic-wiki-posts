So far an estimated 51 different nationalities (by birthplace and including Scotland) have played for Celtic (depending on what you count as a country, or whether you accept those who played for several national teams!). The list below excludes Scottish players as there are too many, and also includes some who never made a first team performance. It is also incomplete so please do add anyone you spot as missing or rectify any other errors!.
