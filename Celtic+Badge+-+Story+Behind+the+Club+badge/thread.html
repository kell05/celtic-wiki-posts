By: WeeShamrock, Jul 18 2009, 4:48 PM EDT
I really llike this story but I'd seen mention before of Celtic having used the 4 leaf clover much earlier than this story suggests although I hadn't seen any proof until today.

In 1908 the directors gave each player a medal to celebrate winning all four trophies that season (League and 3 cups). It is almost exactly the four leaf as we know it, with 'Celtic Football Club 1908' around the circumference. It's a very impressive design.

A photo of it is contained in the book about Jimmy Hay by his grandson Roy 'James Dun Hay, 1881-1940 The Story of a Footballer'. Will try and get it posted.
