Match | 1980-81 Pictures | 1980 - 1981 | 80's Pictures | Year By Year Pictures | Picture Library



1980 European Cup Winners' Cup match at Celtic Park, Celtic v DVTK of Hungary, this is Frank McGarvey scoring Celtic's 4th Goal, as Davie Provan looks on.




1980 European Cup Winners' Cup match at Celtic Park, Celtic v DVTK of Hungary, this is Frank McGarvey scoring Celtic's 3rd Goal, as Tom
McAdam looks on.




1980 European Cup Winners' Cup match at Celtic Park, Celtic v DVTK of Hungary, this is George McCluskey scoring Celtic's 6th Goal, as Dom sullivan and Murdo MacLeod looks on.
