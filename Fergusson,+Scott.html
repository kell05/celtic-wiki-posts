Fergusson, Scott

F | Player Pics | A-Z of Players | Players who Never Played a Game

Personal

aka: Scott Fergusson
Born: 26 March 1976
Birthplace: Toronto, Canada
Signed: X-Form May 1992
Height: 5' 7'' (170cm)
Position: Midfielder
Internationals: Scotland
International Caps: 0
International Goals: 0

Biog


Canadian-born midfielder raised in the Edinburgh area and co-opted into the Celtic Boy's Club fold in late 1990 after spells with his local Salvesen and Tynecastle BCs.

Played U18 stuff in the Scottish Amateur Youth League, Glasgow Cup and BP Youth Cup throughout 1992/93 on youth forms, but subsequently accepted a pro contract offer from nearer-to-home Meadowbank Thistle on 26 October 1993. Failed to establish himself as a first team player at Meadowbank and was no longer on the books at the time of their metamorphosis into Livingston in summer 1995.

Possibly played Junior or East of Scotland League football after his departure but his later activities are unknown.
