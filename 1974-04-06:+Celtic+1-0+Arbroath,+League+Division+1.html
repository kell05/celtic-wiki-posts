1974-04-06: Celtic 1-0 Arbroath, League Division 1

Match Pictures | Matches: 1973 - 1974 | 1973-1974 Pictures

Trivia

  • Back in came Danny McGrain at right back with Davie Hay going to the left and Jim Brogan on the subs bench.
  • Celtic........27-43
  • Rangers...28-39
  • Aberdeen.27-35
  • Hibs.........25-35

  • Abba win the Eurovision song contest with the song 'Waterloo'.
  • On the same day Celtic drew 3-3 with Arbroath in a Reserve League Match at Gayfield. The Celtic team was Williams, Quinn, O'Hara, McNamara, McDonald, Welsh, McLaughlin, Bone, Ritchie, McCool, Lynch. Subs J. Davidson and Casey. The Celtic scorers were Lynch, Bone and Ritchie.

Review

Dalglish grabs the vital winner with a fine header in the second half.
Celtic huffed and puffed over this one. Eventually Davie Hay swapped positions with Callaghan and things started to happen. The move for the goal started from Jinky and through to Steve Murray who crossed from the left and Dalglish headed home. Harry Hood had to be subbed by Paul Wilson after an injury.
