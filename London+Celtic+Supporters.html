ClaireCroydon Old Fox and Hounds - New Home to the Bhoys of Croydon 0 Aug 25 2012, 1:01 AM EDT by ClaireCroydon
Thread started: Aug 25 2012, 1:01 AM EDT  Watch
With our old home the Broad Green Tavern temporarily closed - we have a new club room upstairs at the Old Fox and Hounds Pub - on London Road, opposite West Croydon station. Kids still welcome. Details of our televised matches can be found on our website.


Directions
From West Croydon station - exit and cross the road.
Nearest tram stop: West Croydon station
Bus: Lots including 109, 250, 60, 50 198, 64, 119 stop directly outside the pub but any bus to West Croydon Bus Garage will take you within a minutes walk.
