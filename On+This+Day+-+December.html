On This Day - December

On This Day

December

1st 1974: Simon Donnelly is born in Glasgow
2nd 1911: Patsy Gallacher makes his Celtic debut in a 3-1 home league win over St Mirren.
3rd 1938: Celtic defeat Hibernian 5-4 in thrilling league clash at Celtic Park
4th 1951: Jock Stein signs for Celtic from Llanelly.

1982: Celtic lift the League Cup after a 2-1 win over Rangers at Hampden.
5th 1973: Harry Hood hits a hat-trick as Celtic beat Rangers 3-1 in the League Cup semi-final at Hampden Park.
6th 1966: Celtic sign Willie Wallace from Hearts for a fee of £30,000
7th 1966: Celtic beat Nantes 3-1 in Glasgow to qualify for the quarter-final of the European Cup.
8th 1888: Celtic 9-2 Clyde in the Scottish Cup 5th Rd

1951: Jock Stein makes his Celtic debut in a 2-1 home league win over St Mirren.

1979: Celtic's game at Dens Park is postponed. Some fans make their way back to Hampden where Aberdeen draw 0-0 with Dundee United in the League Cup final.
9th 1933: Celtic's Peter Scarff dies at the age of 25 after suffering from TB.
10th 1997: Celtic sign Harald Brattbakk from Rosenborg
11th 1974: A crowd of 30,000 attend Parkhead to watch Celtic take on Benfica for the Children's Cup (UNICEF Cup). This one-off fund-raising match is won by the Portugese side on penalties after an exciting 3-3 draw. An impressive £20,000 was raised for charity on the night.
12th 1880: All time Celtic great Jimmy Hay born in Beith, Ayrshire
13th 1958: John Colrain grabs a hat-trick as Celtic win home league tie with Stirling Albion 7-3!
14th 1971: Tommy Gemmell moves to Nottingham Forest for a fee of £35,000
15th 1888: Celtic 2-1 East Stirlingshire in the Scottish Cup 6th Rd
16th 1950: John McPhail scores a goal after only six seconds in a 6-2 victory over East Fife at Celtic Park.

2000: On-loan defender Ramon Vega scores twice on his Hoops debut as the Bhoys thrash Aberden 6-0 at parkhead.
17th 1921: Celtic thump Clydebank 6-0 at Parkhead
18th 1971: Celtic defeat Motherwell 5-1 in league clash at Fir Park
19th 1931: Jimmy McGrory scores four goals as Celtic win league tie 6-0 at Leith Athletic. The game would be Peter Scarff's last for the Hoops.
20th 2009: Ten men Celtic take the lead but end up 2-1 losers in a league clash with Hearts at Tynecastle.
21st 1935: Jimmy McGrory hits his fourth hat-trick of the season as Celtic defeat Aberdeen 5-3 in league clash at Parkhead.
22nd 1893: Celtic win 5-4 in a league clash at Dumbarton.
23rd 1978: Ex-Celt Andy Ritchie scores the only goal as Celtic lose against Morton at Cappielow. This would be Celtic's last league game until March 3rd 1979.
24th 1955: Celtic defeat Hibernian 3-2 in a league clash at Easter Road.
25th 1965: Celtic thrash Morton 8-1 at Parkhead in a league tie.

1971: Celtic defeat Hearts 3-2 at Celtic Park in the last league fixture to have ever been played on Christmas day. *In total Celtic have played 15 times on Christmas day winning thirteen with one draw and a single loss.
26th 1883: Alec McNair born

1935: Stevie Chalmers born in Glasgow.
27th 1969: Celtic thrash Partick Thistle 8-1 in a league clash at Parkhead.

1980: Celtic lose heavily by 4-1 at Pittodrie against Aberdeen. The team were 4-0 down when Charlie Nicholas scored the consolation and gets the biggest roar of the day from the huge faithful Celtic support.

1982: Charlie Nicholas scores his 30th goal of the season in the 5-1 home win over Morton on bank holiday Monday. He wins a prize from the Daily Record in the process for being the first Scottish player to hit 30 goals that season.
28th 1901: Celtic defeat Kilmarnock 4-2 in a league clash at Parkhead.
29th 1979: Rangers 1-1 Celtic at Ibrox in Celtic's last game of the decade. Bobby Lennox has the distinction over scoring the last Celtic goal of the 70's.
30th 1961: Charlie Nicholas born in Cowcaddens
31st 1898: Celtic travel south of the Clde to take on Third Lanark in a league tie the Bhoys win 4-2
