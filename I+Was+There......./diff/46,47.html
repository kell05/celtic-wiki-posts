Articles

'I Was There...' is a look at some of the most memorable and great games in Celtic history as seen through the eyes of the support.

Fans who attended these many memorable matches have taken the time to share their personal experience of these historic clashes. It is hoped the Question and Answer sessions for each game will provide an alternative take on some classic encounters from that presented by more detached press reports.

The recollections present a unique and personal insight not only to these momentous games but also to life as a Celtic fan.
