Player of the month 2009/10
August

3 MOTM performances wins the August Award for Aiden McGeady.

Named MOTM against Aberdeen and both games against Arsenal, a 2nd place at home to Dinamo Moscow and a 3rd place against St Johnstone easily kept him ahead of second placed Landry N'Guemo.




Result on KDS
September
2 MOTM performances in the last two games of September was enough for Paddy McCourt to win the KDS Player of the Month for Sep '09.

Named MOTM away to Falkirk in the League Cup and then away to St Mirren in the League, he also managed to nab his first two goals for Celtic with one in each of these matches.


Result on KDS
October
Andreas Hinkel only played in the last three of the seven matches played this month, and was MOTM in ALL THREE.
More than enough to make sure he was named the KDS Player of the Month for October





Result on KDS
November

Aiden McGeady and Landry N'Guemo both collected 6 MOTM points this month and so are named Joint Player of the Month Winners for November '09

Result on KDS
December
Three MOTM Awards helps Aiden McGeady to win the Player of the Month for Dec-09

Result on KDS
January
Four top 3 places including 2 MOTM Awards gives Marc Antoine Fortuné the Player of the Month title for Jan-10.

Result on KDS
February
Fortuné's good form in January carried over into February with 2 MOTM awards against Kilmarnock and Hearts making him the Feb-2010, KDS Player of the Month for the second month in a row.

New signings Braafheid, Kamara, Rasmussen, Keane and Rogne all managed to get MOTM points this month too.




(Result on KDS)
March
Robbie Keane's 8 goals in 5 games and 3 MOTM shows helped him to lift the KDS Player of the Month for March








(Result on KDS)
April
Two MOTM performances in the two victories over Hibernian, a second place in last week's win at Tannadice, and a third place in the victory against Motherwell who just happen to be this weeks opponents.
That was enough to give Aiden McGeady the Player of the Month for April






(Result on KDS)
May
Only 3 games played in May meant a 4-way split at the top of the May Player of the Month Table.

In alphabetical order Brown, McGeady, Naylor and Zheng topped the table as Joint Players of the Month for May.
