As a club we are pretty fortunate to have our own first season ticket out there somewhere. Could not have picked a better name from our history, well mibee Walfrid or even John Glass. Though i say we could not have picked a better name, i have had a theory that this ticket did not belong to our first Honorary President and it actually belonged to his father, well i would like to think it was, given that he died less than 3 weeks after we kicked our 1st baw. heory is that the ticket would have said Dr. John Conway and not just plain Mr. Also, although we have no evidence that Doctor John's father was actually involved in the founding of Celtic, we do read that he was one of the most spirited Catholics of his day.
