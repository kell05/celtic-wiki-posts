The top photograph shows Celtic on board the ferry on their European tour. Look at the CRACK in the photograph, and focus on the slightly built man wearing a light coloured hat and a bit to the left. He was Geirge Graham at that time emerging as a clerk/secretary in the SFA. Later he initiated the furore over the Eire flag at Celtic Park in 1952, and was granted a knighthood "for services to football".
