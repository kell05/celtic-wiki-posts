September 2008
There were 6 Games played this month...

13-Sep-08 12:30 Motherwell 2-4 Celtic SPL Samaras - Brown S - Maloney
17-Sep-08 19:45 Celtic 0-0 Aalborg BK CL Samaras - Caldwell - Brown S
21-Sep-08 16:00 Killie 1-3 Celtic SPL Samaras - Caldwell - Nakamura
23-Sep-08 19:45 Celtic 4-0 Livingston CIS Samaras - McGeady - Loovens
27-Sep-08 15:00 Celtic 3-2 Aberdeen SPL JVoH - McGeady - Samaras
30-Sep-08 19:45 Villareal 1-0 Celtic CL Brown S - Caldwell - Boruc
Never mind the Clydesdale Bank awards, this is the one the players look out for.

With 4 MOTM performances in the first 4 games of September...

The KDS Player of the month for September '08 is Georgios Samaras...Result on KDS
