Summary

CelticNewsNow.com was created in October 2010 as a unique way for Celtic fans to read all the latest Celtic News and Celtic FC Blogs from across the web. If you'd like to promote your blog to thousands of daily visitors, send an email via CelticNewsNow.com/about or get in touch on Twitter and Facebook.

Review

A good site that is a simple one stop shop to find the latest news, rumours and stories that are floating about and making the Celtic exclusives all over the web and beyond.

Rips the articles & links from a variety of news sources, and puts it all into a very tidy and accessible format which all will admire and appreciate.

Worth a visit, and if you yourself have a blog to add to their site then feel free to contact them for assistance.

Reprint of Celtic Supporters Historical Songbook (limited edition)

Celtic & Ireland in Song and Story by Raymond Daly & Derek Warfield


BOOK REVIEWS

This is a truly excellent publication, very informative, interesting and accurate with a wealth of detail about both Celtic and Ireland in its 472 pages. It is a very fine book. No Celtic supporter should be without it.

David Potter (Celtic Historian)

This is a book which now takes pride of place in my collection. It is truly a superb and very readable source of information on both Celtic and Ireland`s history.

Big Kev, Fife.

Celtic and Ireland in Song and Story is one of the more important books about Celtic, published in recent years. If you've ever wondered who Michael and Mary were, who was Brave O'Hanlon, what is a Broad Black Brimmer or Sam Brown Belt, and why father exactly was so sad one Easter morn, this is the book for you. A definite purchase and something you'll go back to year after year.

David Hillier

The book starts with the lyrics of plenty of old favourites and some newer ones, after this it goes into real detail from everything from heroes of Irish history to football to music.Illustrated throughout, it serves as a great reference and a valuable addition to the Celtic library. Highly recommended.

Fergal Toomey, Mid-Leinster CSC

Celtic & Ireland in Song and Story book is an excellant focus on historical stories and songs with ballads too. And when you add the key issue's in certain events in history, this makes for exceptional reading. A book that is a must for all Celtic fans.

Glasgow Programmes
