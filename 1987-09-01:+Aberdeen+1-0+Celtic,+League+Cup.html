1987-09-01: Aberdeen 1-0 Celtic, League Cup

Match Pictures | Matches: 1987 - 1988 | 1987-88 Pictures | 80's Pictures

Trivia

  • McNeill chases a striker and Celtic are linked with moves for Ian Baird of Leeds and John Hewitt of Aberdeen.
  • Celtic rage after tribunal awards them £850,000 fee from Man Utd for Brian McClair.
  • Billy McNeill refuses to confirm or deny that Celtic have bid for Charlie Nicholas, now out of favour at Arsenal
  • Alex Ferguson in attendance, watching Aberdeen's Joe Miller, who had also been watched by Kenny Dalglish in the previous round
  • Future Celtic players Joe Miller, John Hewitt and Willie Falconer involved for Aberdeen
  • Maurice Johnston in an interview with Chick Young in the Evening Times expresses his desire to one day re-join Celtic

Review

Celtic's fine build up work counted for nothing and they were toothless up front.
Jim Leighton makes an outstanding save from a Paul McStay shot about 60 seconds before Aberdeen's goal
Bett's fine goal was a worthy winner and Celtic never looked like retrieving the game. McNeill promises more moves in the transfer nmarket.

Teams

Celtic:
McKnight, Morris, Rogan, Aitken, Whyte, Grant, Stark, McStay, McGhee, Walker, Burns
Subs: Shepherd, McGugan


Aberdeen:
Leighton, McKimmie, Robertson, Conner, Irvine, W. Miller, Hewitt, Bett, J. Miller, Nicholas, Hackett
Subs: Grant, Falconer
Scorer: Bett (60)


Referee: J. McCluskey
