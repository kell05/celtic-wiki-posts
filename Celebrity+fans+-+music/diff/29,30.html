The BibleCodeSundays

  • The London Irish band consist of (from left to right) Andy Nolan, Carlton Hunt, Ronan McManus, Enda Mulloy, Kian Chanter and Patrick Franklin. Accordionist/songwriter Andy Nolan is one of the co founders of The Bobby Murdoch CSC in Hayes, West London also known as The Hayes Bhoys. He is also a screenwriter and his feature film Clan London is currently in pre production. Lead singer/songwriter Ronan McManus is the younger brother of Elvis Costello (Declan McManus) while Carlton Hunt was the drummer for ska band Bad Manners. They have played on the pitch at Celtic Park on numerous occasions including the Celtic V Barcelona Champions League game on 7 November 2012. Their song 'They Built Paradise' is a tribute to Celtic's glorious and proud history.
  • Link: http://www.biblecodesundays.com/

Jenny Lewis

Singer Celtic secret



14 October 2008
Provided by: Daily Star

US indie star Jenny Lewis has revealed she is a closet Celtic fan.

The Rilo Kiley singer is the current girlfriend of Scottish country rock star Johnathan Rice.

And she has admitted falling in love with "stylish" Glasgow.

Former child actress Lewis, who has appeared in Baywatch, said: "I love playing Glasgow - it's one of my favourite places to play.

"Johnathan is a proud Scot. And I guess by default it means I support Celtic."
