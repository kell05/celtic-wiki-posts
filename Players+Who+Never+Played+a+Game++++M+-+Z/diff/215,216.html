Pat O'Hara 1963-64 1963-64

Tommy O'Hara's older brother Pat was on Celtic's groundstaff in 1963. Farmed out to Maryhill and later Glenafton, Shotts Bon Accord, Kirkintilloch Rob Roy and Shettleston. Lacked sufficient pace to be a senior and gradually drifted backwards from left winger to left midfield to left back. Despite his lack of pace he read the game brilliantly and passed the ball with great accuracy. Capped several times for Junior Scotland while with Rob Roy and Shettleston. Last heard of playing amateur football in his native Uddingston in the late 1980s. Sported a goatee beard.
