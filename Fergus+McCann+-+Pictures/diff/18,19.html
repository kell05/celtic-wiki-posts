Fergus McCann married Elspeth Campbell on 27th January, 1995.


From Celtic View February 1, 1996.

"It was a day to remember for Celtic MD Fergus McCann last Friday
For he tied the knot with Glasgow lawyer Elspeth Campbell. The happy
couple were married in St Margaret's Church, Ayrshire.
They met while Elspeth was working at Celtic Park last March.
Celtic fans wich them every happiness in their new life together.
