1988-08-24: Celtic 7-2 Hamilton Academicals, League Cup

Match Pictures | Matches: 1988 - 1989 | 1988-1989 Pictures

Trivia



Review

Played on a balmy August evening, the game was virtually dead and buried by half-time, Andy Walker's third minute goal coupled with his second on the half hour mark and Frank MacAvennie's goal on 37 put Celtic 3-0 up at half time, Tommy Burns made it four and Frankie Bhoy grabbed his second to make it 5-0 before 60 minutes had elapsed but the Accies rallied slightly and a quick-fire double by Jamie Fairlie(?) sent alarm bells ringing in the Hoops defence (justifiably so because three days later we went to Mordor and lost 5-1), Billy Stark netted number six then Owen Archdeacon scored the seventh.


Teams

Celtic: Andrews, Morris, Rogan, Aitken, McCarthy, Grant, Miller (Archdeacon),, McStay, McAvennie, Walker, Burns
Hamilton Accies: Ferguson, McKee, Kerr, McNaught, Jamieson, Collins, Fairlie, Harris, Scott, Roseburgh, McDonald.
Referee:
Attendance: 23,190
