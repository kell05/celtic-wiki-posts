The Celtic Way. 6th May 2014.

I’m off to Paradise to see
The changes made to history
Our meeting place was taken away
It’s now The Celtic Way.

Though history will always last
It’s sometimes good to change the past
The old school’s gone and have to say
I love The Celtic Way.

It’s all brand new and lined with trees
Our fans are looking awfy pleased
And the Celtic Crest’s destined to stay
On The Celtic Way.

The Commonwealth will have its games
And Parkhead’s used to famous names
And all the world will see that day
The (famous) Celtic Way.

Have you seen it lit up at night
My God it’s such a wondrous sight
It’s Paradise both night and day
It’s called The Celtic Way.

The Holy Poet.
