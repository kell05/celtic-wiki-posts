Celtic Team Line-Up 1965-66

Matches: 1965 1966 | Pictures: 1965-66 | Team Line-ups 1964-65 - 1966-67









Glasgow Celtic
Young, Gemmell, Fallon, Murdoch, McNeill, Clark.
Chalmers, Gallacher, Hughes, Lennox, Auld.




Back Row L to R
Connelly, Gemmell, Young, Cushley, Divers, Chalmers, Gallacher, Jim Brogan.

Middle Row L to R
Stein, Hughes, McNeill, Bent Martin, Jack Kennedy, Simpson, John Fallon, McCarron, Murdoch, O'Neill, Sean Fallon.

Front Row L to R
Bobby Rooney, Johnstone, McBride, Quinn, Cattenach, Clark, Auld, Lennox, Mochan.

Trophies L to R
Glasgow Cup, St Mungo Cup, League Cup, Scottish Cup, Coronation Cup, Glasgow Exhibition Cup. Missing - League Championship trophy (probably not yet awarded - also Scottish Cup was lost to Rangers in 1966), and European Cup Winners Cup - stolen in Liverpool!
