11. Jimmy McStay (1929-1934)
12. Bobby Hogg (1934-1935)
13. Willie Lyon (1935-1939)
14. John McPhail (1948-1952)

Trivia

  • James Kelly, signed from Renton, was the club’s first captain.
  • Willie and Jimmy McStay were brothers and Paul McStay was their great-nephew.
