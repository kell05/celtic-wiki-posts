US born (Mexican descent) Manny Perez was a very surprise signing at the end of the January transfer window and completely out of the blue, another one of those prospects for the long-term future. Perez had represented USA at U20 level and has been named Player of the Year in the USA Soccer Development Academy.

He was loaned back straight to return to North Carolina FC for further experience after his signing, so the supporters had to wait to find out what he could do for Celtic.

Five facts about new Celtic signing Manny Perez

https://www.hitc.com/en-gb/2019/02/01/five-facts-about-new-celtic-signing-manny-perez/

Celtic announced a surprise deal for talented prospect Manny Perez on deadline day. The deal for Perez was confirmed by the club as part of a double announcement with Andrew Gutman. Perez had been on trial at Celtic in December,
Here are five facts about the new Celtic addition... 1) Perez impressed in the collegiate system in America. He scored five goals and provided four assists in 15 games for North Carolina. 2) The 19-year-old has been a regular recently for the USA under-20 side, making six appearances and scoring once. 3) Perez also had the opportunity to play international football for Mexico - but the US were first to express interest in him. 4) Perez rejected the chance to sign with Major League Soccer clubs because he was determined to progress his career abroad. 5) In 2017 Perez won the prestigious ACC US Freshman of the Year award, handed out to top prospects in his region.
