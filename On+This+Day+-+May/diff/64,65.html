May

1st 1950: Danny McGrain born in Glasgow.

1971 : The Lisbon Lions turn out for the last time against Clyde in a 6-1 win against Clyde at Parkhead. Ronnie Simpson leads the team out but cannot play because of injury.
2nd 1997: Tommy Burns dismissed as Celtic manager.
3rd 1975: Billy McNeill makes his final appearance in the Hoops as he skippers Celtic to a 3-1 Scottish Cup final victory over Airdrie at Hampden.

1986: Celtic defeat St Mirren 5-0 at Love Street to clinch the league title from Hearts on goal difference.
4th 1974: Celtic cruise to a 3-0 Scottish Cup final win over Dundee United at Hampden to claim the 'Double'.
5th 2007: Celtic went to Ibrox having already been crowned league champions but duly slumped to a 2-0 defeat.
6th 1970: Celtic lose 2-1 to Feyenoord after extra time in the final of the European Cup at the San Siro, Milan.

1972: Dixie Deans nets a hat-trick as Celtic beat Hibernian 6-1 in the Scottish Cup final at Hampden to claim the 'double'.
7th 1977: Andy Lynch's penalty gives Celtic victory in a 1-0 win over Rangers in the Scottish Cup final.
8th 1953: Celtic sign Neil Mochan from Middlesbrough for a fee of £8,000.
9th 1893: Celtic clinch their first ever League Championship with a 3-1 home victory over Leith Athletic.

1945:Celtic win The Victory in Europe Cup defeating Queens Park in the Final.
10th 1980: A George McCluskey goal in extra time gives Celtc a 1-0 victory over Rangers in the Scottish Cup final.
11th 1953: A Bobby Collins goal gives Celtic a thoroughly deserved 1-0 win over English champions Arsenal in the First Round of the Coronation Cup.

1979: Celtic win 2-0 against St Mirren at Ibrox in a game played on a Friday evening. Love Street was in the process of reconstruction and Police would not allow the game to be played on the Saturday due to Rangers playing Hibs at Hampden. Lennox and McCluskey scored.
12th 1971: Celtic defeat Rangers 2-1 in the Scottish Cup final, after a replay.

1984: Celtic sign Alan McInally from Ayr United.
13th 2000: Mark Fotheringham becomes the youngest player to play a competitive game for Celtic when he debuts in the league clash with St Johnstone. He was aged 16 years and 204 days.
14th 1960: Celtic wear numbers on their shorts for the first time as they defeat Sparta Rotterdam 5-1 in a friendly at Parkhead.


1988: Celtic clinch the 'double' in their centenary season with a 2-1 Scottish Cup final victory at Hampden against Dundee United
15th 1982: Celtic defeat St Mirren 3-0 at Parkhead to clinch a second succesive league title.

1993: Celtic entertain Dundee at Parkhead. The match is the last league game to be played in front of the Jungle terracing. The famous enclosure would be converted to a seated area.

2001: Lisbon Lion Bobby Murdoch died in the Victoria Infirmary
