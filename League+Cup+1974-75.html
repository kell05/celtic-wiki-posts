League Cup 1974-75

Matches: 1974 - 1975 | League Cup | 1974-75 Pictures
This season again the format of the competition was changed. Teams played the normal Group Stage with the top team from the group qualifying. Qualifiers progressed to the Quarter Finals where the teams would play each other Home and Away. Group 9 was a group of 6 teams which played 5 matches before a play off game would leave 16 qualifiers for the Quarter Finals.

Also applying this season
  • Two substitutes were allowed during the game
  • The offside rule as used in the Drybrough Cup was applied - one could only be offside beyond the 18 yard line extended to the touch lines.
