aka: Over and Over Radio Show, OAO Radio Show

Ended: Nov (?)2012
Link: http://www.spreaker.com/page#!/show/oaoradioshow

Details

Over & Over Celtic News includes Radio Show every Friday during the season between 7pm-10pm plus audio bulletins, matchday coverage and live commentary...

[write-up needed]

Review

The OAO radio show is a regular show on Friday evenings during the season which combines discussion of Celtic matches, issues and topical events. A lot of Celtic themed music (traditional, contemporary and modern) fills in between the chat, which includes a lot of discussion with various guests and phone-ins.

Very down-to-earth and informal, this is like listening to your pals on the supporters' buses views on Celtic. Increasingly getting a following esp for those all stuck at home on a Friday night!

Enjoyable and has made it's mark in the growing Celtic spoken word world online.
