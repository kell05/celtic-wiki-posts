1948-09-04: Albion Rovers 3-3 Celtic, League Division A

Match Pictures | Matches: 1948 - 1949 | 1948-1949 Pictures

Trivia

  • An interesting player in the opposition ranks was the centre half, a young John Stein.
  • This was Tommy Bogan's last game for Celtic. On 30th September he was transferred to Preston North End.
