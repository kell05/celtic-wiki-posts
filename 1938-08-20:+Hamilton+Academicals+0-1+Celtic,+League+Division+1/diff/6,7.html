Trivia

  • Though Morgan, Hamilton's keeper, had some great saves a smartly taken goal by Jimmy Delaney after half an hour is enough to give Celtic the points.
  • Champions Celtic lead the league table on goal average with Partick Thistle, Hearts and Clyde also on 4 points.
  • In England as a curtain raiser before the league starts, 44 derby games were played raising £22,000 for the Jubilee Benevolent Fund.
  • The Empire Exhibition in Glasgow highlights the Scottish shale-oil industry, which recently produced over 3,000,000 tons per year.
