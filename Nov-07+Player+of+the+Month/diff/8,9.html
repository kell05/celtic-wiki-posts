November 2007

4 games played.

  • 03-Nov-07 Killie 1 v 2 Celtic SPL - McDonald - McGeady - Hartley
  • 06-Nov-07 Celtic 1 v 0 Benfica CL - McGeady - Hartley - McDonald
  • 25-Nov-07 Celtic 3 v 0 Aberdeen SPL - Brown S - McGeady - Jarosik
  • 28-Nov-07 Celtic 2 v 1 Shaktar Donetsk CL - Jarosik - McGeady - Hartley


Consistency, including a MOTM performance against Benfica was enough for young Aiden McGeady to win the KDS Player of the Month for November, result on KDS
