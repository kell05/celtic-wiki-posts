Collectables | About Celtic

Details

At one time collectors items used to be things like coins and the like. From around the nineties or so, up sprang the little bobblehead like toys, which are meant to represent footballers. Not much good as toys for the kids, but they're nice ornaments and some have a penchant for collecting these.

The below pics shows one fans great collection of these items, generally sold under the Superstars or Pro All Stars branding. A fun novelty look at our footballing heroes.

If there is one obvious curiosity then it's that the figures are meant to resemble the players in question. A quick gloss over them proves otherwise even to the most trained eye! It's not meant to be Madame Tussaud's level of quality anyhow, and it is just harmless humour with these.

Enjoy the collection below and see for yourself how many of the figures you can identify yourself.

From HajraFradi1888 of KDS Forum






A lot of them are unique and the only ones in existence.

- Di Canio in bumbles with gold boots and base
- Billy McNeill in late 60's away top
- Dani in a Celtic top
- Bobby Charlton in a Celtic top
