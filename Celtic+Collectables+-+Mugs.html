Celtic Collectables - Mugs

Collectables | About Celtic

Tea & Coffee Mug Collection

We’ve all received them as gifts at Xmas, birthdays or leaving do’s, the “Mug” is probably one of the most universally given items. Cheap, simple and well-loved, out of all the gifts we are given, the mug will be the most used item by a long shot, and will likely be the longest lasting.

The mugs are printed with wonderful designs often, catering to fans of all ages. Also they are wonderful for commemorating great moments in our club's history (e.g. Seville 2003).

For anyone who questions the sentimentality and usefulness of the mugs, just here's an idea. Next time you get a fan of another club (e.g. Sevco Huns) come over and they are annoying, make them a cup of tea and put it in that mug and hand it to them. Just see the reaction next. A big dilemma as you can't say no to a cuppa!

Here is a wonderful walk down memory lane of the evolution of designs on mugs. Enjoy!
