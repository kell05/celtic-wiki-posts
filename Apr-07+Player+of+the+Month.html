April 2007

4 games played.

  • 07-Apr-07 Celtic 1 v 0 Motherwell SPL McGeady - Riordan - Nakamura
  • 14-Apr-07 Celtic 2 v 1 St Johnstone Scottish Cup Semi Final JVoH - Naylor - Riordan
  • 22-Apr-07 Killie 1 v 2 Celtic SPL Nakamura - Kennedy - JVoH
  • 29-Apr-07 Celtic 1 v 3 Hearts SPL Doumbe - Jarosik - Naylor

For the first time, we have joint winners as Jan Vennegoor of Hesselink & Shunsuke Namamura share the Player of the Month for April.



1st= JVoH 4
1st= Nakamura 4
