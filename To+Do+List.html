To Do List

This is admittedly well out of date...

Our editors have beavered away for many weeks to come up with the framework that you see before you, but there is still a long, long way to go. If you want to help, but you're not sure where to start, you may want to think about these items in our To Do List.

High Priority

[started]

  • Match reports for every game in the Year by Year section.
  • Scanned Programmes for every game in the Year by Year section.
  • Player names and profiles in the complete A to Z section.
  • League Cup- Round by round details
  • Details, dates, scores etc of Glasgow Cup games from 1948 up to when the Cup stopped being contested by senior players; additions to the Glasgow Cup write up in Tournaments section also required.

[Not Started]
  • Player names and profiles for reserves and youth team players

Other Priorities


Fans
  • Celts who aren't Kelts (write ups wanted from fans who are Celts but are not of Scots or Irish origin and experience of Celtic and the respective community)
  • Article on Jungle Bhoys

Scotland
  • Celtic players and Scotland National Team
  • Celtic players and Ireland National Team

Events/tales/history
  • Tiger Tim getting the sack as Celtic announcer...
  • Celtic and great refereeing moments!
  • Celtic and bad refereeing moments!

    We enjoyed the benefit of the doubt in this one!

  • Other sports that have been played at Celtic Park...

Non Celts
  • St Pauli write up and their links with us..
  • Superstars (Cruyff/Best etc) who appeared at Celtic Park

In fact, absolutely anything to do with Celtic. We want your facts, articles, statistics and opinions. Find your section with either the SEARCH facility (top left), or click on the appropriate link on the left and get editing.

Good luck.
