November 2008
There were 8 games played this month...

02-Nov-08 Hearts 0-2 Celtic SPL Robson - Brown S - Hinkel
05-Nov-08 Celtic 1-1 Man Utd CL Caldwell - McManus - Brown S
08-Nov-08 Celtic 2-0 Motherwell SPL Brown S - Hartley - McDonald
12-Nov-08 Celtic 3-0 Kilie SPL Sheridan - Donati - Hinkel
16-Nov-08 Hamilton 1-2 Celtic SPL Hartley - Brown S - Robson
22-Nov-08 StMirrenSt Mirren 1-3 Celtic SPL Hinkel - Nakamura - Samaras
25-Nov-08 Aalborg 2-1 Celtic CL Robson - Caldwell - Brown S
29-Nov-08 Celtic 1-0 ICT SPL Hinkel - Hartley - Nakamura
Scott Brown, for the 2nd month in a row, is the the KDS Player of the Month.

In November we played 8 games and Scott made the top three in five of them, getting named MOTM in the 2-0 win over Motherwell on the 8th. He also had two 2nd places and two in 3rd.

There were other good performers in November, Andreas Hinkel and Barry Robson both won MOTM twice, while Hartley, Caldwell and Sheridan got the other three MOTM Awards. 23 Players were used this month. (Result on KDS)
