Trivia

  • Dundalk state that next week's EC tie against Celtic will be played at Oriel Park and not Lansdowne Road Dublin.
  • New signing Don Sullivan is cup tied. tied.George McCluskey is out after suffering an injury in the Rangers game. Johnny Doyle was fit again with Tommy Burns injured and Edvaldsson kept his place after coming on as a sub against Rangers.
  • Steve Archibald scores a rare hat trick against Celtic.

Review

Steve Archibald scored a sensational hat trick to put himself in the £1M bracket at Pittodrie. It was a perfect hat trick of right foot, left foot and header and he was a handful for the Celtic defenders for the 90 minutes.

Celtic had made a sensational start by scoring in the first minute. Alan Sneddon raced to the bye line and cut the ball back for Johannes Edvaldsson to crash the ball past Bobby Clark.

Archibald then took a hand in proceedings with his three goals, the pick of the bunch the third with a ferocious shot past Latchford.

Davie Provan gave Celtic hope for the second leg when he scoedscored from a brilliant swerving free kick into the top right hand corner in 70 minutes.
