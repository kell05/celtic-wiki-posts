Tales form TheCelticWiki

Since 2010 the Celtic Underground podcasts have featured a regular article from this site to help popularise the club's history.

"The Tales from TheCelticWiki" segment features varied events, stories and anecdotes from throughout the rich history of Celtic as featured on this website.

The tales were drafted and read out by TheCelticWiki site moderator TheHumanTorpedo accompanied by a perfect backing tune titled "The Sun Smells Too Loud" by Glasgow cult band "Mogwai".

You can find the tales at the end of respective Celtic Underground Podcasts (see: link).

The following are a collection of some of the tales done. We hope you enjoy them.

History of Celtic Audio Podcasts by TheLostBhoys

In conjunction with various folk from online Celtic forums & sites, in 2013 TheLostBhoys have put together a series of free podcasts on the history of Celtic. Each podcast has a knowledgeable set of guests who helps guide us through the events, trials and successes of Celtic through the respective period.

The podcasts were created for the benefit of the support, asking in return only for voluntary charitable contributions of which the entire amount donated will be divided between the charities of choice of the contributors to the podcasts.

CelticUnderground Nine in a row podcasts

Wonderful set of podcasts on Celtic's Nine-in-a-row seasons, plus the one that got away.

Recorded in 2020 during the COVID-19 shutdown period.

Link: https://celticunderground.libsyn.com/webpage/category/podcasts
