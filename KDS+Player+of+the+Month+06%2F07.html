August 2006



Aiden McGeady is the KDS Player of the Month for August.















September 2006



Just like last month, Aiden McGeady wins again and is the KDS Player of the Month for September.
















October 2006

2 MOTM displays, a 2nd place and 2 third places wins Shunsuke Nakamura the KDS Player of the Month for October.















November 2006


A terrific month for "Gnasher" as Lee Naylor wins the KDS Player of the Month for November.











December 2006


Another great month and Shunsuke Nakamura wins the KDS Player of the Month for December.





















January 2007


What a season he's having as Shunsuke Nakamura wins the KDS Player of the Month for January.



February 2007

Can no-one stop him as Shunsuke Nakamura again wins the KDS Player of the Month for February.






















March 2007

2 MOTM performances sees Aiden McGeady win the KDS Player of the Month for March.














April 2007


For the first time, we have joint winners as Jan Vennegoor of Hesselink & Shunsuke Namamura share the Player of the Month for April.

May 2007


Joint Winners again as Artur Boruc & Jean-Joel Perrier-Doumbe share the KDS Player of the Month for May.
