1977-11-16: Celtic 2-0 St Mirren, League Cup

Match Pictures | Matches: 1977 - 1978 | 1977-1978 Pictures

Trivia

  • Frank Munro returned to England at the start of the week to think over the terms he was offered by Celtic for a permanent deal. He returned and signed another 1 month deal.
  • Danny McGrain returned to hospital for further tests on his injured ankle and heel.
  • Ronnie Glavin was out injured.
  • Second leg - Celtic win 5-1 on aggregate
  • Young Celt Peter Mackie makes his full debut at right back. He is the fourth player to play in that position since McGrain was injured six weeks ago - Kay, Aitken and Fillipi being the first three.
  • Hearts beat Dundee United on penalties and will play Celtic in the semis at Hampden.
  • England beat Italy 2-0 at Wembley in a WC qualifier.

Review

Celtic win through easily on the night. Paul Wilson has his best game of the season. He scores the first goal with a header from an Andy Lynch free kick and then makes a fine run beating two men for Doyle to score the second.

Celtic fans chanted 'Fergie, Fergie shut your mouth', towards Saints manager Alex Ferguson.
