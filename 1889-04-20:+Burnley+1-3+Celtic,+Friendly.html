1889-04-20: Burnley 1-3 Celtic, Friendly

Match Pictures | Matches: 1888 - 1889 | 1887-1900 | Forum



Trivia

  • Friendly match, played at Turf Moor, Burnley.
  • Before 5,000 spectators.
  • This was Celtics second of two games in two days, to be played in England over the Easter weekend.


Review

Played at Burnley. The Celtic scored in the first minute, and had three before the interval. Burnley put on one in the seconf half, and Celtic won by three to one.


Teams

Burnley:-
Cox, White, Berry, McFetteridge, Lang, Keenan, Yates, Brady, McKay, Duckworth, Grant.

Goal:-
Duckworth.

Celtic:-
John Kelly, McLaren, McKeown, Jas Kelly, W Maley, Dowds, Dowling, Dunbar, Groves, Coleman, Naughton.

Goals:-
Groves, Unknown, Unknown.

Att:- 5,000.
