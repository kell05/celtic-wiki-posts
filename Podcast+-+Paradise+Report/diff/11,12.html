Paradise Report Podcast

Started: Nov 2010
Type: Podcast
Presenter(s): Krys Kujawa
RSS Link: http://www.spreaker.com/show/hail_hail_media
Links: You will find the respective audio reports for the matches on this wiki site, see the match page and go to the "Match Report" section on the page for the downloadable mp3 link.

Synopsis

Paradise Report is an "as live" podcast recorded at Celtic matches. From the pre-match build up to brief post-match analysis, KK reports on all the action on the park from within the atmosphere off the park.

Having started as a section of the LostBhoys podcast at the 9-0 thrashing of Aberdeen in November 2010, the match reports grew to the point of being put out as podcasts in their own right towards the end of the title winning 2011/12 season. 2012/13 sees the podcast continue to expand, becoming a fully fledged member of the ever-growing Hail Hail Media platform and bringing live recordings from as many Celtic matches as possible as well as hopefully other future events, including those organised by the Celtic Graves Society.

Additionally, KK brings us his thoughts in the Paradise Report blog on the variety of locations the Celtic supporters visit while following the team; from Celtic Park itself to various other football grounds.

Review

(by joebloggscity)

Note: You will find the respective audio reports for the matches on this wiki site, see the match page and go to the "Match Report" section on the page for the downloadable mp3 link.

Many of you probably already know Krys quite well. A popular contributor on Celtic forums and article sites since the earliest days (mostly under the "Psychoheart" moniker) he has made a little name for himself.

In season 2010/11, he branched out bit to create a new niche in the online Celtic world with his audio reports. They comprise of highlights of matchesmatches, compiled whilstby Krys giving commentary by speaking into hisa phonemicrophone in his jacket. The sillier he may look to those around him on the terraces the better the podcast usually is!

These have become absolute gems and an incredible repository of the high and low moments a supporter feels during the game.respective match (and we don't half get taken on a roller-coaster in some games). Krys' great voice itself lends perfectly to the whole experience and is an added bonus for the listener.

It's not just matches that are commented on but Krys has done other events as well, such as audio commentaries fromon the Celtic Graves Society remembrances. These reports are of great valueinterest and are done to a high standard. For those of us unable to attend, Krys' reports are a perfect substitute.

Started originally as a post fix to the LostBhoys podcasts in Nov 2010, it has now got its own separate podcasts under team title 'Paradise Report' available under the "Hail Hail" media RSS feed. Very well deserved.

Hopefully Krys' reports will continue to be a regular addition to the online world for seasons to come. A truly wonderful addition to the blossoming Celtic online world.
