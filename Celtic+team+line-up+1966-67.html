Celtic Team Line-Up 1966-67

Matches: 1966 1967 | Pictures: 1966-67 | Team Line-ups: 1965-66 1967-68





BACK ROW - Jim Brogan, Willie O'Neill, Ian Young, John Cushley, Tommy Gemmell, Jim Craig, Davie Cattenach, Charlie Gallacher, Tony Taylor (the winger who also played for Crystal Palace), Jim Clark.

MIDDLE ROW - John Higgins (Coach), Neil Mochan, Brian Goodwin, Jimmy Quinn, John Clark, John Hughes, Bent Martin, John Fallon, Ronnie Simpson, John("Jack") Kennedy, Frank McCarron, John Halpin, Sam Henderson, Davie Hay, Bob Rooney, Sean Fallon

FRONT ROW - Jimmy McGrory, Tom Devlin, John Taggart, Jimmy Johnstone, Steve Chalmers, Joe McBride, Billy McNeill, Bob Kelly (before he was Sir Bob), Bobby Murdoch, Bobby Lennox, Bertie Auld, Lou Macari, John Gorman, Desmond White, Jock Stein.

The photo was taken at the start of the 1966-67 season.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------






Pay Attention!!! - Listen to The Boss!!!

The majority of the Lisbon Lions Squad at training with Celtic Manager Jock Stein.

Standing Lto R - Jock Stein (Manager),John Hughes,Stevie Chalmers,Ian Young (Non Lion),Bertie Auld,Billy McNeill and John Clark.

Kneeling L to R - Bobby Lennox.Tommy Gemmell,Bobby Murdoch and John Fallon.

**This picture is slightly unusual in that Jock Stein is at Barrowfield wearing a suit as Jock was a "Track Suit Manager" and was involved in the day to day training of the players.
