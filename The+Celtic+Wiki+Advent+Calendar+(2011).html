The Celtic Wiki Advent Calendar (2011)

The Celtic Wiki Advent Calendar
The Celtic Wiki Advent Calendar is a collection of 24 classic photographs and images which aim to encapsulate the spirit and incredible history of Celtic Football Club.

From December 1st until Christmas Eve an image will be revealed each day via a new page on the Wiki. The page for each December day up to Christmas Eve will be linked to the site's Twitter and Facebook accounts for followers and friends to 'open'. By December 24th the advent calendar will comprise of an evocative collection of iconic Celtic images. Some of the images will stir memories of great triumphs and heroes, while others will capture the fun and pride of following and playing for the Hoops.

Most importantly it is hoped The Celtic Wiki Advent Calendar will simply provide a fun countdown to Christmas for fans of the Bhoys across the world!
